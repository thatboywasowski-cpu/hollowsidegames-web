About the Studio

We're building Hollowside Games.

Hollowside Games is growing into a studio ecosystem built around our games, HollowEngine, and a long-term creative identity that feels cohesive across tools, web, and player-facing experiences.

Current Focus

  • Building the Hollowside web portal and account system foundation.
  • Growing HollowEngine into a full creative platform.
  • Preparing long-term tools, publishing, and studio infrastructure.

Studio Values

What we want the work to feel like.

Identity

We want the studio, engine, and games to feel like one connected world, not separate disconnected pieces.

Ambition

We're building with long-term systems in mind early, even while the platform is still being shaped.

Craft

We care about feel, presentation, and experience - not just whether something technically works.
